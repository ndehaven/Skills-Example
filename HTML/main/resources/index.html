Rhetorical Technique: Comparison
Showing the contrast in negative and positive colors for each of the pie charts in the games when Jimmy Butler is playing and not playing leads the viewer to believe that the more positive outcomes(wins) the better the team is playing.

Rhetorical Technique: Anchoring
Anchoring the W/L ratio of the games with and without Jimmy in a manner where if the ratio is negative will appear negative and if the win/loss ratio is positive will appear with a positive in front of it is a narrative technique to catch the user's attention with additional details to sway their opinion
